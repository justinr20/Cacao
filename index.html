WELCOME

Cacao Essence Beauty was born from my personal journey with psoriasis and sensitive skin. After years of strugling to find to find products that truly nourished my skin with out irritation, I decided to create what I couldn't find.

I am a registered nurse with over 10 years of experience, and I've seen firsthand how what we put on our skin matters. Combining my medical background with my passion for natural healing, I began formulating products that are gentle, effective, and rooted in nature.

At the heart of every product is certified organic cacao sourced from a family farm in Arroyo Toro, Dominican Republic. Cacao is rich in antioxidants, deeply moisturizing, and known to repair sensitive, dry, and compromised skin. Each product is thoughtfully crafted with minimal ingredients, using only natural and organic componets. Cacao Essence Beauty is more than skincare, it's a return to simplicity, tradition, and intentional self-care.
